Christmas Wishes is a responsive festive website I built to send holiday greetings to close friends. The goal was simple: a warm, visually appealing page that felt personal and fun to receive.

I started from a template I found online, then modified the code to match my own style, adjusting the layout, colors, and copy until it felt like mine. The final result is a clean, mobile-friendly holiday page hosted on Vercel.

This was a small personal project but a good reminder that polishing something simple to feel genuinely nice takes more care than it looks.
